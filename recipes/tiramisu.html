Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a timeless no-bake Italian dessert combining espresso-dipped ladyfingers and a creamy lightly sweetened mascarpone cream. This recipe is from the late Maida Heatter and is easily the best homemade tiramisu recipe that Iâ€™ve ever tried.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Espresso
  • Grand Marnier
  • Mascarpone
  • Rum
  • Egg Yolks

Steps

  1. Dip half of the ladyfingers in the espresso mixture
  2. Beat mascarpone and rum together
  3. Beat egg yolks into mascarpone cream mixture
  4. Fold egg white mixture into mascarpone cream mixture
  5. Layer half of the cream mixture on top of the ladyfingers
